How to create an NFT?

  1. Go to https://app.charcoin.io (coming soon)
  2. Click 'Connect Wallet' and choose an appropriate option
  3. Make sure you have enough Eth on the account to cover transaction costs (gas fees) - this depends on network congestion
  4. Go to 'Create'
  5. Upload your creation, add title and description
  6. Click "Submit my creation"
